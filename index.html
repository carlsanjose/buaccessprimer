About Us

Our Mission:

The BU Academic Consortium of Computer Science Students strives to reinforce the structure of today's future thinkers to endow the capacity of tomorrow's envisioned innovators for a revolutionized future.

Our Vision:

An organization that upholds the welfare of its constituents through significant and sustainable experiences that cultivate efficient, reliable, goal-oriented, and future-ready individuals.

Our Objectives:

  1. Strive to promote, improve, and develop among students increased knowledge and greater interest and understanding in computer and information technology.
  2. Encourage and promote continuing research and study, and may adopt other measures as necessary, to foster education and advancement in the field of computer technology.
  3. Serve as a vessel for computer technology awareness and consciousness through intellectual inquiry and information exchange among its members; and
  4. Strive to meet a high degree of academic standards and technical excellence in the field of computer technology through its members.

Kumsay Klash 2026

Event Description

The Bicol University Academic Consortium of Computer Science Students (BU ACCeSS), in collaboration with the Bicol University Polangui Association of Computer Science Students (BUP ACSciS), presents Kumsay Klash ‚Äî an engaging event full of competitions and a workshop held in celebration of the National Science, Technology, and Innovation Week. This event serves as an avenue for the Computer Science community of Bicol University to showcase their talents and skills The initiative fosters healthy competition, academic exchange, and professional networking among aspiring technologists and innovators across the region. The two-day event features a series of intellectually stimulating and skill-based activities, including the Computer Programming Competition (CPC), Esports Tournament (featuring Mobile Legends: Bang Bang and Call of Duty: Mobile), Quiz Bee, a Robotics Workshop, and Ideathon. These sub-events serve as platforms for participants to demonstrate technical proficiency, creativity, and collaboration, while strengthening camaraderie and institutional partnerships among Computer Science students from various universities.

Objectives

This event intends to celebrate the talents, skills, and camaraderie of Computer Science students through a series of competitive and collaborative events that promote technical excellence, creativity, innovation, and community spirit among participants from Bicol University and other institutions.

  • Provide a platform for students to demonstrate their knowledge and skills in programming, problem-solving, and critical thinking through competitive activities such as the Computer Programming Competition (CPC) and Kumsay Quiz Bee.
  • Foster teamwork, strategic thinking, and sportsmanship through Esports tournaments featuring Mobile Legends: Bang Bang and Call of Duty: Mobile.
  • Enhance technical proficiency and innovation through the Robotics Workshop, encouraging students to explore emerging technologies and practical applications in automation.
  • Strengthen collaboration, partnership, and unity among Computer Science communities across universities, promoting a culture of continuous learning, innovation, and professional growth.
  • Align the event with the Sustainable Development Goals ‚Äî SDG 4 (Quality Education), SDG 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth), SDG 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure), and SDG 17 (Partnerships for the Goals) ‚Äî by fostering education, innovation, collaboration, and opportunities for future-ready skills development.
